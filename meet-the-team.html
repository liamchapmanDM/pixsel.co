The Team

Jesse Todd - Creative Director

Jesse is a specialist in all things photo & video, who has been working in the real estate industry with a focus on media since 2016, both in-house and freelance. He has a great wealth of knowledge in marketing properties and has worked on thousands of listings. Jesse has a passion for content and loves creating new and fresh ideas to generate interest which lead to faster sales. He has previously worked on social content for multiple large household brands and graduated in 2018 with a degree in Digital Film Production from Ravensbourne University London.

Sam Butcher - Drone Operator

Sam has a real passion for photography and film, as well as being a highly skilled drone pilot. Sam is able to shoot incredible cinematic aerial footage and maintains a 100% safety record since obtaining his BNUC-S qualification through Euro USC in 2015. He is PfCO (PFAW) licensed and has a true eye for capturing beautiful aerial imagery.
